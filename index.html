Thank you for expressing interest in the sixteenth edition of mimamsa! Mimamsa is an attempt by the students of IISER Pune to propogate the simple yet profound idea of critical thinking, while also popularizing the pure sciences among the undergraduates of India. It is truly a one of its kind contest when it comes to pure sciences. A year long effort goes into making the questions that we use. Constructed and fine tuned by our team, each question intends to encourage digging deeper into already known concepts and by the end, improving your undertstanding.


The contest is set to happen in two seperate stages: prelims followed by mains. Teams of 3 to 4 students with members from the same college and in the 1st, 2nd and 3rd years of their respective undergraduate course can enrol for the prelims. Top four teams according to the prelims will be invited to IISER Pune for the Mains which will be a 3 day ordeal involving various quizing events to test the finalist's abilities to determine who the next champions of mimamsa will be.


So what are you waiting for? Hurry up and take the first step towards being inquizitive by filling up the form!
